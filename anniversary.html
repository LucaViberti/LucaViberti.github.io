Anniversary Realm

The Anniversary Realm is a cooperative guild event where all members can move across the event map, defeat enemies, and gather both personal and collective rewards.

Each player starts with 10 free attacks per day, and can purchase more if needed. You can fight enemies in different areas of the map, with varying difficulties. It's recommended to use the Hard difficulty against stronger foes to maximize damage and rewards.

As players clear enemy zones, the central boss for that area will be unlocked. Defeating this boss grants huge rewards for the guild. While some rewards are given immediately, many are stored in a shared Guild Vault and will be distributed at the end of the event.
