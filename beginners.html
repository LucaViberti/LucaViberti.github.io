Beginner Guide

Just started Top Heroes? This guide keeps things simple, helps you avoid common mistakes, and shows what to do first to grow fast.

1) Introduction & First Steps

When you start, follow the Adventure path and avoid wasting key resources. Two golden rules:

  • Don’t spend meat on Rare (blue) heroes — they won’t be used later.
  • Don’t use diamonds for speed-ups early — timers are short, diamonds are precious.

2) Village & Buildings

Once you reach your Village, upgrades become less guided. Aim to rush the Castle to level 20–25 to unlock essential buildings and higher hero/soldier levels. Keep Research Cottage, Hospital, Guild, and Training Ground paced with Castle requirements. Remember: Barracks promote soldiers every 5 levels — a big power bump for your queues. For details, see Buildings. [oai_citation:0‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

3) Join a Guild (ASAP)

From Castle 7 you can join a Guild. Try for a top 3 (or at least top 10) guild in your server: active recruiters remove inactives and help you catch up fast.

Help & Speed-ups

Use Guild Help on every construction/research. Request and wait for helps before using speed-ups. Spend speed-ups (building/research/universal) wisely; avoid speeding with diamonds. [oai_citation:1‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

4) Farming Resources

Main resources are Timber, Stone, and Ruby. Best early method: push Adventure; if stuck, improve your lineup and retry.

  • Guild Secret Trial: team mini-levels → extra rewards.
  • Guild Expedition: 1 attempt daily. Use Blessing for buffs + 100 Guild Points (spend in Guild Store).
  • Bounty Hall: missions every 8h; keep them rolling.
  • Expeditions & Stages: unlock over time. [oai_citation:2‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

5) Tavern & Hero Shards

Spend Tavern Tokens freely early to get Shards. Star upgrades are a huge power spike. Don’t spend Legendary Universal Shards early — save them. [oai_citation:3‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

6) Heroes & Factions

Focus on one main queue from a single faction (buffs scale later, especially in Season 1). See Heroes for roles and synergy.

  • League: easy early; costlier mid/late for F2P.
  • Horde: slower start; very strong long-term, often cheaper than League.
  • Nature: slowest early; shines much later. [oai_citation:4‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

7) Spending Real Money

You can play F2P or spend. If you spend, buy Gold Bricks on the official website for a constant +5% bonus and milestone rewards, then use them in-game. See Shop & Codes. [oai_citation:5‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

7.1 Best Purchases

  • Monthly Pass: extra queue (Privilege), Auto Hunt, double ad rewards, daily gifts. Most cost-effective. First milestone via site gives ~30% discount.
  • Remove Ads ($2): permanent; instant ad rewards.
  • Construction Queues: default 1 → buy up to +3 ($2/$10/$20), permanent.
  • Research Queue: default 1 → unlock 2nd for $10, permanent. [oai_citation:6‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)
  • Adventure Begin (Best Price): cheap limited packs (3 versions) over a few days.
  • Packs: daily hero shard packs (~$5) → for bigger spenders.
  • Skins: global buffs; Legendary $30 / Mythic $100 → priority for high spenders. [oai_citation:7‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)
  • Wheel & Dice: alternate weekly; save tickets until Mythic heroes appear.
  • Adventure Battle Pass: optional ($15/$20 or both $30).
  • Funds (Construction/Adventure/Power/Growth): good mid-spender value.
  • Daily Special: choose shards + Star Gems (later even Mythics). [oai_citation:8‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)
  • Daily/Weekly Packs: low value for small spenders; ok for mid/high.
  • Diamond Store: never — same price, fewer extras.
  • VIP Points: come with purchases; nice perks, don’t buy just for VIP. [oai_citation:9‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

8) Events

Early on, focus on Top Lord: just play and raise Influence to earn great rewards (e.g., Griffon Castle skin). Boost Influence by assigning soldiers to idle heroes and upgrading Barracks; Warehouse/Tavern/Watchtower give quick bumps too.

  • World Boss: daily 5 hits, no stamina/losses. Fixed rewards; ranking rewards minor — just do your 5.
  • Ruin Hunt: daily chests on World Map — do all. Don’t pay diamonds to refresh early. Help guildmates (prioritize purple/gold). You can plunder others’ hunts — go for legendary level 4. [oai_citation:10‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

Beginner-only: Beginner Goals, Seven Day, Power Rush — time-limited, don’t miss them. For schedules and rotation, see Events. [oai_citation:11‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

9) Research

Research in the Research Cottage. Early game: complete Combat Expertise 100% to unlock other trees. Then plan with our Research page. [oai_citation:12‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

10) Social & World Chat

World Chat unlocks at Castle 7. It may be messy at first, but moderators clean it up quickly. Invitation codes give small rewards (shards, gear, diamonds) — nice if you have friends, otherwise optional. [oai_citation:13‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

11) Villagers

Save Villagers daily in Village (also appear in Adventure). The first Daily Quest grants a ticket to spawn one. Assign them in Talent Hall to boost buildings. Early on, Quick Dispatch is fine; later, optimize assignments. See Villagers. [oai_citation:14‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

12) Carriage & Plunder

Dispatch 4 Carriages per day: rarities are Common → Mythic. Use the free refresh to hunt Hero Shards. After 5 refreshes you’re guaranteed a Legendary; from Castle 15 there’s a 5% Mythic chance. Legendary/Mythic never downgrade. Target Universal Legendary Shards when possible.

Mythic sharing: can be shared with full rewards and extra protection. Some servers avoid attacking full Mythics (5/5) — propose it if your server doesn’t. Plunder: 4 attacks/day, best-reward targets; failed attacks don’t consume attempts. Each Carriage can be attacked 2 times (3 if Mythic). Check details first. See Carriage. [oai_citation:15‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

13) Guild Traits

In Guild → Traits, members donate to unlock buffs. Donations regen every 30 minutes up to 20 stored — don’t let it cap or regen stops. Diamond donations (10 each) aren’t worth it early. Donations grant Guild Points to spend in the Store. [oai_citation:16‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

14) Stamina

Needed for World Map attacks; it regenerates. Twice daily you can claim 80 stamina (≥4h apart). Monthly Privilege grants 50/day (100 with bonus). 25/50 stacks are fine early; avoid buying 150 stamina for $5 — poor value. [oai_citation:17‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

15) World Map: Enemy, Resources, Rally

  • Enemy: fight Guards (Timber/Stone/Ruby) up to Lv30 — very useful early. Always hit Legend Stash 3 times daily for free rewards.
  • Resources: first capture gives fixed reward. Leaving troops = Gathering. Prioritise Ruby (overnight). Timber/Stone less valuable.
  • Rally: fight bosses with guildmates; great stamina spend. Joining is free and the first 50 give rewards — join as many as possible. [oai_citation:18‡Beginner.docx](file-service://file-C7oyzmPrV4Geeg9xZAG3bm)

16) Final Notes

Top Heroes rewards planning and timing. Early on, you can spend most items freely (except diamonds and Legendary Universal Shards). Later, save and spend during events (e.g., Guild Race, Arms Guild Race) to maximise points — that mindset is key to long-term success.
