Adventure Mode — Classic (Normal & Hard)

Classic Adventure is the foundation of TopHeroes. It introduces key mechanics, grants your first heroes and gear, and sets the stage for all progression.

The Classic Adventure map is where every player begins their journey in TopHeroes. This is your foundation — it introduces key mechanics, provides your first heroes, weapons, and gear, and allows you to gather resources needed for long-term progression.

In Classic mode, you'll fight your way through a series of zones. Each zone ends with a portal boss. Defeating that boss unlocks the next area. Occasionally, you'll also face Adventure Bosses that drop valuable chests when defeated, which can be opened using stamina.

While it's tempting to rush through, it's highly recommended to complete each zone 100%. Doing so will give you bonus rewards and ensure you don’t miss out on decorations or secret chests.

Hidden Decorations

One unique element in Classic Mode is the presence of hidden decorations. These are often subtle objects in the environment that feel out of place. When your heroes walk near them, they'll begin chipping away like they do with rocks, but more slowly. Leave them to work, and after a short while, they’ll collect the decoration.

Collecting all decorations across the Classic map is a great way to get them easily for free.

Unlocking Hard Mode

Once you've completed all zones of the Classic map in Normal mode, you'll unlock the Hard Mode version. This version features the same layout but with significantly stronger enemies and better loot.

In Hard Mode, boss fights become more strategic and challenging. However, they also start to drop legendary gear, making this the best place to start farming for high-end weapons early in the game. The drop rate might seem low, but consistent farming pays off.

Farming Bosses

It’s a good habit to revisit and farm bosses you've already defeated. Boss chests contain gear, shards, and other useful resources. You can open up to 250 chests per day (2500 stamina limit), which is more than enough for daily farming without stress.

If you plan ahead and don’t let stamina overflow, you’ll always have a strong supply of materials coming from Classic.

Classic Mode may seem simple at first, but it's designed to teach you how to play efficiently and reward smart exploration. Use this time to build a solid hero lineup, experiment with gear, and prepare yourself for tougher challenges ahead.
