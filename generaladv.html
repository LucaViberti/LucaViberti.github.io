Adventure Mode — Overview

Adventure is the core PvE mode in TopHeroes. It fuels your early progress with heroes, weapons, and gear, and sets up your entire run.

General Overview

Adventure is the first and most crucial game mode in TopHeroes. It's the main source of initial resources, including heroes, weapons, and gear. Progressing through it is essential to starting your journey the right way.

Structure

The adventure is divided into different zones. To unlock the next zone, players must defeat a portal boss. Occasionally, Adventure Bosses appear and, once defeated, they drop a chest. Opening these chests costs 10 stamina and gives valuable rewards like gear, shards, tavern tokens, diamonds, and more.

Each zone can be completed 100% by finding all hidden paths, secret chests, and helping soldiers. Completing a zone fully grants additional bonuses. This is highly recommended before moving to the next area.

Adventure Maps

Players who start at server launch will initially only have access to the Classic map in Normal mode. After completing it, the Hard Mode of the same map will unlock. Later, with Season 1, a new map and extended levels in Classic Hard will be added.

Once you've cleared all available maps, it's a good time to use leftover stamina to farm previous bosses and complete 100% zones.

Chest Icon (Bottom Left)

There’s a chest icon in the bottom left that fills passively over time, even when you’re not actively playing. You can claim it anytime without any penalty. It stops accumulating after 6 hours if unclaimed — so check it periodically.

As you progress in adventure, this chest levels up and gives slightly better rewards. There’s also a clock icon that offers you the rewards of 2 hours in exchange for 20 stamina. This is not worth it — save your stamina for better things.

Progression Tips

  • At the beginning, focus on pushing adventure until you get stuck.
  • Farming bosses repeatedly is a good strategy to maximise rewards and collect gear and shards.
  • The daily stamina limit for opening boss chests is 2500 — very high and rarely reached unless you're playing intensely.
  • As you progress, boss rewards improve significantly, especially in the Classic Hard Mode, where legendary weapons begin to drop.
  • Many of these weapons are purple tier; for how to use them effectively, refer to the Gear section under Heroes.
