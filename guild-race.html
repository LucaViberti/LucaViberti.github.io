Overview

Guild race is one of the main events in the game. Try to get as many honour points as possible. The honour shop is one of the best shops in the game.

You get points for finishing tasks. At the start you get 9 task tickets for free and you can buy 15 more every day. We recommend you buy at least the first 5 task tickets every day because they are really cheap (300 diamonds). The following 10 task tickets cost each 300 dias. You can buy these task tickets as well when you have plenty of tasks (best only golden) to finish. It’s really worth buying more because the honour shop is great. We would recommend your guild to let people know they should only take tasks if they plan to finish them really fast. If one person is blocking, for example, the soldiers task for hours, he is slowing down the whole guild.

You have 2 exclusive tasks only you can use. Every 10 minutes you can refresh one of the exclusive tasks for free. If you want, you can use 100 diamonds to refresh it without waiting for the cooldown. We don’t recommend doing that: the costs and chance to get the task you need are not worth the diamonds. Better buy more task tickets. Most of the exclusive tasks need less resources to finish (e.g., exclusive soldiers task needs 400 trained; the guild task needs 500). You can even prepare tasks and keep one over server reset by accepting it before reset (you can abandon it later and it will remain in your exclusive slot).

Most of the time you need luck to get the task you need. That’s why it’s not good to rely only on exclusive tasks. Find the best route to finish as many gold tasks as possible. If you’re willing to spend, Task selection tickets help a lot (use them only on golden tasks).

Tasks you should use in guild race

  • Soldiers training task (ONLY GOLD. Very easy; usually 2/day).
  • Building task (ONLY GOLD. Non ritirare le costruzioni: aspettare l’esclusiva d’oro).
  • Research task (early-mid più difficile; in late una singola ricerca può bastare. Prepara in anticipo per non sprecare tempo).
  • Diamond spending (meglio esclusiva, idealmente d’oro; sincronizza con acquisti settimanali/quotidiani).
  • Buy Diamonds from packs (se spendi soldi: solo esclusiva; se < 1.6k diamanti totali, meglio un viola altrove).
  • Initiate rally / kill bosses (solo viola; usa se hai molti ticket).
  • Kill mobs on world map (solo viola; il più facile e senza risorse, anche joinando i rally).

A bit later in the early game

A bit later in the early game, you will get these only in exclusive tasks. They give slightly more points than usual golden tasks:

  • Consume Legendary Relic Shards (Day 3–4, ONLY GOLD. Conservale e usale qui).
  • Consume Magic Stones (Day 3–4, ONLY GOLD. Puoi anche scambiarle per Arcane Crystals nello shop risorse).
  • Use Medals (Day 5–7, ONLY GOLD. Conservale per GR; eccezione: shop medaglie nelle settimane senza GR per speed-ups).
  • Increase Power of Lords gear (Day 5–7, ONLY GOLD. Sincronizza i set bonus 3/6 pezzi: il power spike spesso chiude un task da solo).

Tip: Arrivare a 3 o 6 pezzi allo stesso livello dà un boost di potere che può chiudere un task; poi alza un singolo pezzo per massimizzare.

Tasks you can finish but better save for Arms Race / Kingdom vs Kingdom

  • Use Research/Construction speed-ups (costo alto per i punti: spesso non conviene in GR).
  • Consume Legendary Shards (meglio conservarli per Arms Race / KvK, a meno di upgrade urgenti).

Tasks you should NEVER use

  • Recruit hero in the tavern (solo viola e poco efficiente; meglio farmare mobs per pari punti. Conserva i tickets taverna per Arms/KvK).

Rewards

You also get rewards for individual and guild honour points each week. Puntare ad almeno 7,000 punti personali (prima di S1 il cap era 6,000). Con un po’ di impegno si superano facilmente i 10k. Comprando almeno i primi 5 ticket/die, arrivi a ~44 task settimanali.

Season 1 research

With Season 1, in Shadow of the World Tree ci sono due upgrade chiave per la Guild Race:

1) Extra rewards per 6,500 e 7,000 honour points (individual) e 65,000 e 70,000 (guild).

2) Honour gain boost qualche nodo più in basso nell’albero: aumenta i punti ottenuti per ogni task completato.

As soon as possible, cerca di sbloccare entrambi.

Honour shop: cosa comprare

Alcuni acquisti diventano importanti più avanti nel gioco:

  • Soul Stones — 6,000 honour (sempre quelle della tua main faction; è la fonte principale F2P).
  • Universal troop skin — Legendary 12,000 / Epic 1,200.
  • Legendary Universal Hero Shards — 300 ciascuna.
  • Universal exclusive gear shards — 400 ciascuna.
  • Legendary Universal Relic Shards — 800 ciascuna.
  • Dragon Essence — 120 ciascuna (late game ne serviranno molte; buono acquisto ricorrente).
