HordeDesert Prince

Tank /Damage Dealer

Skills:

  • Royal Armor: Grants a Shield that absorbs Damage
  • Sand´s Blessing: Gains additional HP and deals % of max HP as true Damage
  • Royal Treasury: Increase skill damage for all allies when Royal Armor activated
  • Scorching Scimitar: Deals true damage
  • Eye of Insight: Reduce Skill Damage taken for this hero
  • Enthusiast: each Legendary or higher Horde hero in this queue increases enemy´s death and wounded ratio

Dessert Prince is on oft the best heroes in game. He is a tank that deals great damage as well. This hero is a must have in every horde queue. As soon as you get him you should replace swordmaster or barbarian for him.

Recomandation: We would recomend you to replace swordmaster (if barbarian around same star lv) for him. Your horde team will lack in sustain at this point. Barbarian would help you more in that case as swordmaster.
