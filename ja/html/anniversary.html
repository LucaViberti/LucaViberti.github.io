Daily Login Reward

During the entire Anniversary Celebration, don't forget to visit the official Top Heroes website every day to claim your daily login reward.

Simply log in using your UID code, then scroll down the page to find your reward. It only takes a few seconds!

Anniversary Realm

The Anniversary Realm is a cooperative guild event where all members can move across the event map, defeat enemies, and gather both personal and collective rewards.

Each player starts with 10 free attacks per day, and can purchase more if needed. You can fight enemies in different areas of the map, with varying difficulties. It's recommended to use the Hard difficulty against stronger foes to maximize damage and rewards.

As players clear enemy zones, the central boss for that area will be unlocked. Defeating this boss grants huge rewards for the guild. While some rewards are given immediately, many are stored in a shared Guild Vault and will be distributed at the end of the event.

Toyland Store

Throughout the event, you'll collect Random Toy Material Bags. Opening one will give you a random material used to build toys.

Head over to the Toyland Store to use those materials and construct toys. Each day, one toy will be marked with a demand icon. Building the requested toy will grant you bonus points and extra rewards. Make sure to focus only on toys that have active demand ‚Äî this changes daily.

There's only one exception: the Carousel. It has no demand requirement, so you can build it whenever you need.

On the left side of the screen, you'll find the Material Trade button. Here you can exchange materials with your guildmates.

If you obtain a Toy Material Choice Bag, consider saving it until the final days of the event ‚Äî or at least until the end of the day ‚Äî so you know exactly which pieces you're missing. This helps you score the most points possible.

Fun Gift Machine

The Fun Gift Machine lets you spin for prizes ‚Äî and it comes with a twist: the Lucky Icon. This icon shows you exactly what you'll receive on your next spin.

To maximize 2nd Anniversary Tokens, we recommend spinning one time at a time until the Lucky Icon shows either:

  • The Anniversary Token
  • The Celebration Deluxe Chest

Once you see one of these as the Lucky Icon, switch to the 5-spin mode to collect them faster and more efficiently.

Ideally, use this system to focus on tokens ‚Äî but keep in mind that even the Celebration Chest can contain some tokens randomly.

Anniversary Highlights

In the Anniversary Celebration screen, players can access a special interaction by tapping on the icon labeled "Anniversary Highlights" at the bottom of the page.

Once inside, you'll be greeted by the cheerful tavern hostess. By simply clicking to toast with her, you'll receive a small reward once per day.
