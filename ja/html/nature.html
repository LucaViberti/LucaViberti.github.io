Nature

Nature is a mixed lineup. It shows excellent hero power progression and has the best support hero in the game. Nature's top-tier heroes include Tidecaller, Pixie, and Monk, making it a solid choice for end-game strategies. The lineup is flexible and blends support, damage, and survivability.

Best Lineup

Main lineup: Front: Monk, Altar Marshal | Middle: Pixie, Petalis | Back: Forest Maiden, Tidecaller.

Damage focus lineup: Front: Monk, Altar Marshal | Middle: Pixie, Windwalker or Ne Zha when available | Back: Tidecaller, Forest Maiden.

Notes: Tidecaller is arguably the strongest hero in the game (including all factions). Pixie is viable end-game. Forest Maiden is a good healer later. Windwalker is a powerful damage dealer but generally replaced by Ne Zha. Treeguard/Druid/Sage can be used early but should be replaced.

Nature Hero Breakdown

Treeguard

Most basic Nature tank. Relatively low stats and outdated skillset.

Use: Starter unit. Replace by mid-game with Monk or Sage.

Druid

Nature's early healer. Solid at the start but falls off.

Use: Early to mid-game healer. Replace with Tidecaller.

Windwalker

Strong damage dealer. Reliable front-line damage option.

Use: Good until replaced by Ne Zha or with Pixie focus.

Forest Maiden

Provides healing and support buffs. Weak early but strong end-game.

Use: Situational. Good late-game in many lineups.

Pixie

Incredible Nature damage dealer. Remains viable late into end-game.

Use: Core. Investment-heavy but worth it.

Sage

Moderate tankiness. Decent mid-game tank until replaced by Monk.

Use: Reliable until you get Monk or better.

Watcher

Weak damage and lackluster utility. Not recommended.

Use: Avoid investing. Bench or fodder.

Monk (Mythic)

Best Nature tank. Extremely high sustain, damage mitigation, and utility.

Use: Best-in-slot tank. Priority investment.

Skills:

  • Mountain Strike – AOE with knockback + shield
  • Ultimate Zen Power – Shields self + Rage boost

Tidecaller (Mythic)

Arguably strongest hero in the entire game. Incredibly versatile healer and support. Works in almost every lineup.

Use: Auto-include. Top priority investment for all players.

Skills:

  • Flowing Stream – Heal + shield low-HP allies
  • Ocean's Blessing – Revive ally + continuous healing

Tidecaller is staple in Nature builds and a top choice in multi-faction teams.
