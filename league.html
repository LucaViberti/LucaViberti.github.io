League — Legendary & Mythic Heroes

Curated picks for League heroes: roles, core skills, and when to include or replace them.

Pyromancer

Damage Dealer (Legendary)
Skills
  • Detonate: Deals damage and applies burn.
  • Meteor Blaze: Area damage.
  • Ember: When HP is low, gains an attack boost.
  • Flame Shield: Grants a shield and damages nearby enemies.
  • Elemental Mastery: Increases skill damage.

Your strongest early-game League DPS. Keep her in your League lineup; still usable later on.

Secret Keeper

Tank
Skills
  • Arcane Shield: Increases damage reduction and removes debuffs.
  • Forbidden Realm: Silences enemies and deals damage.
  • Arcane Link: Increases HP.
  • Spell Reflection: Deals damage and becomes immune to damage briefly.
  • Arcane Guardian: Grants a shield that absorbs damage.

First Legendary League tank. Good early; later, replace with Rose Princess if you already run Adjudicator.

Astrologer

Damage Dealer
Skills
  • Ray Beam: Damage with increased crit rate.
  • Solar Beam: Multiple hits.
  • Foresight: Increases crit rate.
  • Arc Light: Damage that can further increase damage dealt.
  • Insights: Increases crit damage.

Great early single-target DPS (Guild/World Boss). Later, prefer Paragon and Bishop.

Hostess

Tank
Skills
  • Evasive Technique: Enters counter state; gains damage reduction, deals damage, and restores HP.
  • 100T Hammer!: Damage and stun.
  • Agile Footwork: Periodically dodges one instance of damage.
  • Drunken Brawl: Damage reduction, healing, damage, and stun.
  • Inn Keeper: Increases team ATK/HP even when not deployed.

Decent early tank with utility. Replace once you get Adjudicator or Rose Princess.

Bard

Support
Skills
  • Cheer: Increases movement and attack speed; may deal damage.
  • Resonant Chord: Deals damage and stuns.
  • Endgame Serenade: After Resonant Chord, reduces allies’ skill cooldowns.
  • Grace: Increases crit rate; may deal damage.
  • Fight Song: Increases crit rate for League heroes.

Good support: speed, crit, and cooldown reduction. Can fit even into end‑game teams.

Nun

Healer
Skills
  • Therapy: Heals an ally and increases healing received.
  • Radiant Song: Increases allies’ max HP and final damage reduction for a time.
  • Holy Grace: Grants self damage reduction.
  • Prayer of Healing: Heals all allies.
  • Sacred Union: Increases max HP of League heroes.

Your main healer from early to late game. Keep her in your League lineup.

Adjudicator

Tank
Skills
  • Guardian Discipline: Damage, stun, damage reduction, and control immunity for a time.
  • Shield of Faith: Summons a massive shield; on expiry, deals damage.
  • Indomitable: Grants damage reduction to all allies.
  • Judgment: Damage, stun, and self‑healing.
  • Eternal: Near-death invincibility; HP cannot drop below 1 during the effect.

One of the best tanks in the game. Scales very well with stars. Staple of end‑game League teams.

Paragon

Damage Dealer
Skills
  • Commanding Call: Deals damage; grants allies a shield.
  • Celestial Blade: Control immunity, damage, applies burn stacks; gains damage reduction.
  • Final Verdict: Increased damage against enemies above 70% HP.
  • Moral Fury: Raises allies’ attack and deals damage.
  • Ultimate Execution: Extends attack range and adds an extra target; boosts normal attack damage.
  • Conqueror: Each Legendary+ League hero boosts castle attack; with 6 League heroes, gains skill crit rate.

Usually your primary League DPS. Replace Astrologer with her once acquired.

Rose Princess

Tank / Damage Dealer
Skills
  • Be Brave: Gains stacks of Courage (damage reduction). Taking damage converts a stack to Resentment.
  • Bloom: Consumes Resentment stacks to deal damage and apply Heal Ban.
  • Thorns: Deals damage; extra damage per Resentment stack.
  • Weapon Up!: Damage reduction scales with her ATK.
  • Roses: Gains Courage and HP when enemies die.
  • Guardian Messenger: Increases soldiers’ HP per 1000 deployed.

Starts modest but becomes a top League hero with stars — a tank that also hits very hard.

Bishop

Damage Dealer
Skills
  • Fair Strike: Extra effective versus summoned units.
  • Divine Descent: Damage; increases ATK and damage reduction; normal attacks become AoE for a time.
  • Solar Shield: Increases crit and crit resistance.
  • Confession: Deals damage and applies Weaken (reduces amplified skill damage), strong vs summons.
  • Sacred Burst: Triggers AoE damage when enemies die.
  • War Messenger: Increases soldiers’ attack per 1000 deployed.

High DPS slightly below Paragon on average, but still end‑game worthy — include alongside Paragon where possible.
