Lord’s Gear

With Castle level 24, Lord’s Gear becomes available. This system can provide huge buffs if managed carefully — don’t just upgrade randomly!

Overview

Lord’s Gear gives powerful stats to your account. In the first row, you have Nature Lord Gear; in the second row, League Lord Gear; and in the third row, Horde Lord Gear.

Upgrade Materials

  • Refined Metal: farmed from daily bosses. Some special bosses (like the Tree Boss instead of the Pig Boss) drop extra — excellent moments to stockpile. Early on it feels abundant, but later you’ll need thousands, so always farm it when available.
  • Magic Tread: obtained from many sources, especially Invasion events, but also some stores and events.
  • Orichalcum: unlocked after Rare 2. Much harder to find; comes mostly from events and special shops.
  • Dragon Blood: required only at Mythic level. Very rare, available in big events (like anniversary event shops). Don’t worry about it early — it’s a late-game resource.

Set Bonuses

You get boosts when you have 3 and 6 Lord’s Gear pieces at the same level. These boosts are far stronger than normal upgrades and apply account-wide (not just to one faction). Because of this:

  • Keep all Lord’s Gear roughly at the same level.
  • Ideally have 3 pieces at one level and the other 3 one level below.

Reminder: You get a huge Influence upgrade when you reach a new boost level. During Guild Races, if you have the Lord’s Gear task, you can often complete it with a single upgrade when you’re right at a boost threshold.
