Lords Gear

Overview, materials, farming tips, and how to upgrade efficiently.

Availability and General Idea

With Castle level 24, Lords gear becomes available. Lords gear can provide a huge buff. You can gain an advantage if you don’t upgrade it randomly; a bit of planning goes a long way.

Rows and Factions

Gear is displayed in rows by faction:

  • First row: Nature Lords gear
  • Second row: League Lords gear
  • Third row: Horde Lords gear

Upgrade Materials

To upgrade your Lords gear you will need different materials as you progress through rarities.

Refined Metal

Required from the start. On some special days, instead of the usual pig boss, special bosses (e.g., the tree boss) appear; each boss drops materials. When these bosses are available, it’s an excellent time to farm Refined Metal. Early on it looks like you have too much, but costs ramp up later and you will need thousands, so use those days to stock up.

Magic Thread

Obtainable throughout the game from multiple sources. You can get plenty from Invasion; aim for higher points to receive more threads. It also appears in some stores and events.

Orichalcum

Needed after Rare 2. It’s harder to get than the two materials above; primarily from events and some shops.

Dragon Blood

Required much later (when upgrading to Mythic). It’s the hardest to obtain: typically purchasable only during big events (e.g., anniversary shops). You won’t need it for a long while, so don’t worry about it early.

Set-Level Bonuses (3 & 6 pieces)

You gain powerful boosts when you have 3 and 6 Lords gear pieces at the same upgrade level. These boosts are much stronger than a single piece upgrade and they apply to all heroes (not just one faction).

Because of that, it’s more effective to keep your Lords gear at similar levels rather than pushing a single piece too far. Recommended approach: keep all six pieces roughly aligned, or at least 3 pieces at the same level and the other 3 just one level below.

Reminder: when you reach a new set bonus tier, you also gain a large Influence spike. During guild races with a Lords gear task, you can often finish the task with a single upgrade if that upgrade triggers a new set bonus tier.

Practical Tips

  • Don’t upgrade randomly. Plan your pieces so you hit the 3-piece and 6-piece breakpoints together.
  • Farm special bosses on the designated days to stock up on Refined Metal.
  • Push Invasion points to earn more Magic Thread; keep an eye on events and shops for Orichalcum.
  • Dragon Blood is endgame—take note of big events where it appears, but you won’t need it soon.
