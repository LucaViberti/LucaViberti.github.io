Lords Gear

With Castle lv 24, the Lords gear will be available. Lords gear can give you a huge buff. You can get a bit of an advantage if you don’t just upgrade it randomly. More about that you can read a bit below.

In the first raw, you have Nature gear Lord, in the second raw, you have League, and in the last row, you have Hordes Lord gear.

Upgrade Materials

To upgrade your Lords gear, you need Refined metal, Magic tread, and after Rare 2, you need Orichalcum as well. Much later on when you get your Lords gear to Mythic, you need Dragon Blood as well.

  • Refined Metal: Farm it from special bosses (e.g. tree boss) instead of the pig boss on certain days. Costs will increase a lot later, so farm as much as possible.
  • Magic Tread: Found all around the game, especially from invasion rewards. Also available in shops and events.
  • Orichalcum: Harder to obtain, mainly from events and shops.
  • Dragon Blood: Rarest material, available only in big events like anniversary shops. Needed much later.

Boosts

You get boosts when you have 3 and 6 Lord gear at the same level. These boosts are much stronger than the usual Lord gear upgrade and count for all your heroes and not only one faction.

It’s more effective if all your Lords gear is near the same level. So we would recommend keeping all gear Lord at the same level or at least 3 at the same level and 3 one level under.

Reminder: You get a huge influence upgrade when you reach a new boost level. For guild races, when you have the Lords gear task, you can complete it with only 1 upgrade this way.
