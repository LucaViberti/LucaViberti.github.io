Lords Gear

Overview, materials, farming tips, and how to upgrade efficiently.

Availability and General Idea

With Castle level 24, Lords gear becomes available. Lords gear can provide a huge buff. You can gain an advantage if you don’t upgrade it randomly; a bit of planning goes a long way.

Rows and Factions

Gear is displayed in rows by faction:

  • First row: Nature Lords gear
  • Second row: League Lords gear
  • Third row: Horde Lords gear

Upgrade Materials

Refined Metal

Farm special bosses on their days to stock up. Later you’ll need thousands.

Magic Thread

Earn from Invasion, shops and events. Push points to maximize rewards.

Orichalcum

Needed after Rare 2. Harder to get, mainly events and shops.

Dragon Blood

Required at Mythic. Endgame material, appears in big events only.

Set-Level Bonuses (3 & 6 pieces)

Powerful boosts apply to all heroes. Keep gear aligned to hit 3-piece and 6-piece breakpoints.

Reaching a set bonus tier also gives a big Influence spike for guild races.

Practical Tips

  • Don’t upgrade randomly: plan sets.
  • Farm Refined Metal on boss days.
  • Push Invasion for Magic Thread.
  • Watch for Orichalcum in events/shops.
  • Dragon Blood is only for late game.
