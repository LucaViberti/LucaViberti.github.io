Nature — Legendary & Mythic Heroes

Overview of the main Nature heroes, their skills, and when to use or replace them.

Treeguard

Tank
Skills
  • Growth: Increases max HP, reduces damage taken, and heals self.
  • Timber Shell: Increases damage reduction and max HP, and heals.
  • Vine: While Growth is active, attacks deal extra damage and poison.
  • Titanwood Assault: Summons thorns that deal damage and heal.
  • Vitality Surge: Increases damage and heals.

Solid starting tank with some self-healing. Replace him later with Monk.

Druid

Healer
Skills
  • Nature’s Blessing: Heals one ally.
  • Healing Rain: Heals multiple allies.
  • Force of Nature: Grants a shield that absorbs damage.
  • Nature’s Call: Summons 2 Treemen.
  • Natural Affinity: Boosts Treemen HP.

The starter healer who summons Treemen. Replace with Tidecaller or at least Ne Zha later on.

Windwalker

Damage Dealer
Skills
  • Swift Arrow: Boosts attack speed and normal attack damage, reloads after a number of shots.
  • Windwalker’s Blade: Attacks nearest enemy and blocks incoming damage briefly.
  • Favorable Wind: Normal attacks may not consume Swift Arrow charges.
  • Powerful Ricochet: Deals damage and stuns a target.
  • Preparation: Increases Swift Arrow reload speed.

Your first Nature damage dealer. Replace with Ne Zha later.

Forest Maiden

Support
Skills
  • Baa-Baa Charge: Summons sheep that deal damage and stun enemies.
  • Sheepherd: Summons a lamb.
  • Song of Life: When units die or are sacrificed, all heroes recover some HP.
  • Shepherd’s Bell: Grants damage reduction to nearby allies.
  • Revenge: Each time a unit dies or is sacrificed, all heroes gain permanent damage increase.

A strong support hero that summons units and offers healing. Good even in late game.

Pixie

Damage Dealer
Skills
  • Root Absorption: Summons roots that deal damage and steal attack.
  • Sunflower Core: Summons a core that damages and stuns enemies.
  • Nature’s Harmony: Gains damage reduction for each living Nature hero.
  • Bewildering Powder: Reduces enemy hit rate and deals damage.
  • Forest Ally: Each dead/sacrificed unit reduces Sunflower Core cooldown.

Excellent early damage dealer and remains strong until Mythic heroes. Viable even for end game.

Sage

Tank
Skills
  • Harmonic Balance: Gains partial damage immunity and heals when attacked.
  • Transformation: Transforms into a bear, gaining HP, HP drain, and damage.
  • Power of the Wild: Gains a shield after Transformation that absorbs damage.
  • Serene State: Deals damage, slows attack speed, and removes one buff.
  • Robust Skin: If Sage dies in bear form, reverts with some HP.

The second Nature tank you’ll get. Keep him in your lineup until late game.

Watcher

Damage Dealer
Skills
  • Plant Purge: Creates a whirlwind that damages, poisons, and slows enemies.
  • Wilt: Deals damage and poisons.
  • Slowdown: Normal attacks reduce enemy attack speed.
  • Crow Attack: Gains counterattack, dealing damage and healing when hit.
  • Wheatfield Watcher: Increases damage of Nature heroes.

A weak option overall — not recommended to invest much in. Cannot be awakened and has no exclusive gear.

Monk

Tank
Skills
  • Mountain Strike: Deals damage, increases max HP, and grants damage immunity.
  • Ultimate Zen Power: Grants dodge, damage reduction, and counterattacks when hit.
  • Thundering Kick: Adds a chance to follow up Mountain Strike with a stun.
  • Fiery Breath: Deals damage and grants control immunity.
  • Merciful Victor: Gains a shield when units die or are sacrificed.
  • Guardian Messenger: Increases soldier HP per 1000 deployed.

Your first Mythic Nature hero. A strong tank who deals damage as well. Replace Treeguard with him.

Tidecaller

Damage Dealer
Skills
  • Hydro Hammer: Deals damage, stuns, and summons a guardian water elemental.
  • Ascend: Deals damage, knocks enemies up, and summons Water Lord.
  • Surge: Normal attacks heal all allies.
  • Purification Lance: Deals damage and summons a ranged elemental.
  • Evolve: Normal attacks can hit an additional target.
  • War Messenger: Increases soldier attack per 1000 deployed.

The best Nature hero, and arguably the strongest hero in the game. A must-have for any Nature team.
