Privacy Policy

At TopHeroes Guide, your privacy is important to us. This page explains what data we collect when you use our contact form, how we use it, and your rights.

What data we collect

  • Your email address (to reply to you).
  • The content of your message.

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to respond to your request or feedback. We do not sell, share, or use your data for marketing purposes.

Data storage

Messages are processed via Formspree, the secure service we use to manage our contact form. Formspree temporarily stores submissions to deliver them to our email inbox.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your message and related data at any time by contacting us at guidetopheroes@gmail.com.

Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Last update: August 2025.
