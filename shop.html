Gems & Diamonds

Star gems

This store is refreshed weekly for the first part and daily for the second one.

The purple stars are available from the purchase of the daily special pack or, just a few, from some events like the arms race.

For f2p -> you can accumulate your star points to buy the Legendary Promotion Stone.

Medium/low spenders -> you can also buy the Soul for your main queue

High spenders -> best choice is to buy all the Legendary Promotion Stone every time and as many Soul stones as possible.

In any case, here stay focused on weekly refresh items, the daily refresh can be very good if you are a high spender but for f2p is useless.

VIP

This store is refreshed weekly.

It is based on your VIP level, once reached VIP 10, all the items will be available.

What to buy every week:

  • Relic scroll
  • Pet food
  • Universal speed-ups
  • Stamina

The other things are very expensive so buy that just if you have a lot of diamonds and you need that item for some reason.

Daily

This store is refreshed daily.

It is based on your VIP level, once reached VIP 10, all the items will be available.

What to buy first every day:

  • Premium carriage voucher
  • Shard
  • Token
  • Pet food
  • Stamina

If you can, you can also buy the meat and Gear enhance runce but it requires a high VIP level and many diamonds every day.

The other things are very expensive so buy that just if you have a lot of diamonds and you need that item for some reason.

Relics

This store is refreshed weekly.

Once your relic is at the max of the stars, you can trade it.

You can do that without any problems; all the shards can be used in different ways. Check the Relics tips in order to decide where to invest your shards.

Resources

This store is refreshed daily for the first part and weekly for the second one.

You will lack meat the whole game. So we recommend to trade wood and stone to get more meat.

Weekly you can trade some items based on the traits unlocked by your guild.

You can trade here when you need to, but pay attention to the deal; especially the last line of items is very expensive.

This store can be a weapon during Guild Race and some other events; trade just when you need to, and just during those kinds of events.
