Shop Guide Weekly & Daily Refresh

This page explains what to buy (and what to skip) across all in‑game shops, with quick priorities for F2P, low/medium spenders, and high spenders.

Gems & Diamonds

Refreshed weekly (first part) and daily (second part).

  • Star Gems: Mainly from the daily special pack; a small amount from events like Arms Race.

Priorities by profile

  • F2P: Save star points to buy the Legendary Promotion Stone.
  • Medium/Low spenders: You can also buy the Soul for your main queue.
  • High spenders: Buy all Legendary Promotion Stones every time, then as many Soul Stones as possible.

Daily items are great for whales, but for F2P they are mostly inefficient. Focus on the weekly refresh.

VIP Store

Refreshed weekly. Unlocks fully at VIP 10.

  • Buy every week: Relic Scroll • Pet Food • Universal Speed‑ups • Stamina.

Other items here are expensive—buy only if you’re flush with diamonds or need something specific.

Daily Store

Refreshed daily. Unlocks fully at VIP 10.

  • Buy first: Premium Carriage Voucher • Shard • Token • Pet Food • Stamina.
  • Optional: Meat and Gear Enhance Rune (requires high VIP and many diamonds).

The remaining items are pricey—treat as luxury purchases.

Points Store

Decor

Refreshed weekly. Open this store daily to claim 8 free decor tokens.

  • Save tokens until you reach 500 and buy a Legendary decoration at a discount.
  • One decoration won’t change your game alone—be patient and wait for something useful for your main queue.

Arena

Refreshed weekly.

  • Must‑buy: Diamonds • First Recruitment Token.
  • Very good: Decoration Choice Chests.
  • Recommended: Hero skins (cost ~12,000 Arena points each).
  • Also valuable: Advanced Relocate.

Everything else is expensive—buy only if you have many Challenge Tokens and a clear need.

Medal

Weekly (first part) + Daily (second part).

  • First part: If you don’t have them, buy one Soldier Skin of each type (just once). You’ll later get Epic for free or Legendary with money—save medals too.
  • Second part: Daily buy all speed‑ups; keep resources for emergencies (too expensive for daily use).

Combat Valor

Refreshed weekly. Tokens from Ancient Battlefield & 5v5.

Target: Use these tokens almost exclusively for Legendary Promotion Stone. Don’t dilute on other items.

Guild Store

Guild Points

Daily (first part) + Weekly (second part).

If you’re active you can often buy everything—do it. If you’re new or play less, prioritize:

  • First: Vault Key.
  • Then daily: Refined Metal • Magic Stone • Pet Food.

After a few days you’ll generate more points and can expand to the rest.

Guild Honor

Refreshed weekly. Earned from Guild Race.

  • Best buys: Soul Stones (faction rotates weekly: League → Horde → Nature → repeat; appears around Season 1 start).
  • Universal Exclusive Gear Shards (a few weeks after Season 1 start).
  • Universal Legendary Troop Skin (~25k points).

Focus: Score as many points as possible in Guild Race, then save for the Soul Stones you need.

Reminder: You cannot unlock a Legendary troop skin with the universal Legendary troop skin.

Trade Store

Hero

Refreshed weekly. Once a hero reaches max stars you can trade them. Do this with all blue heroes; Statue use is low value until you’ve maxed the purple heroes as well. For purple heroes you can trade if needed, or keep for Traits/Statue.

Relics

Refreshed weekly. Once a relic reaches max stars you can trade it safely. Spend shards as needed—follow the Relics tips to decide where to invest.

Resources

Daily (first part) + Weekly (second part).

  • You’ll lack Meat all game—trade Wood and Stone into Meat.
  • Weekly trades depend on guild traits.
  • Check the deal value—last row items are often overpriced.

This store counts as resources spent—very handy during Guild Race and similar events. Time your trades accordingly.
