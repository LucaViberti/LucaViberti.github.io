Villagers

You can add up to 4 villagers to most buildings to give extra buffs. Some buffs are not really worth it, while others make a huge difference. Sometimes villagers have 2 buffs for the same building — you’ll see it with a rainbow edge. If they have at least one buff you need, use them.

You can level villagers’ skills by consuming others with the same skill. You’ll lose the consumed villagers permanently, but the chosen villager’s skill increases. Always try to collect all 4 villagers for a building before sacrificing any for leveling. If you see an arrow, you have enough villagers to use as material.

To see all villagers and buffs, visit our Tips & More page.

Mines

Mines villagers are arguably the best in the game if you use yield increase villagers. They can boost your mine output 2–3x. ONLY put yield increase villagers in your mines. If you don’t have them yet, use storage-time villagers until you do.

Blacksmith / Research Cottage / Barracks / Troop Skin Center

Focus only on percentage speed increase. All other villagers are not worth using in these buildings.

Hospital

Healing speed increase sounds strong early, but with Season 1 you’ll face more wounded soldiers. If you plan to save soldiers for Arms Race or KvK, hospital capacity villagers are also valuable. We recommend using a mix of both from Season 1 onwards.

Training Ground

The percentage increase in training capacity is much better, especially at high Training Ground levels.

Tavern

Use villagers that increase the chance of gaining an additional villager per day. The free hero reduction buff is weak — the time saved is negligible.

Watchtower

PvP marching speed bonus is the best option here. It’s not game-changing, but it can make a small difference in world map fights.

Hero Statue

Hero Statue buffs are weak. If needed, use HP villagers if you lack sustain, or ATK villagers if you need more damage. Early game: flat ATK/HP is better; later: percentage buffs scale better.

Medal Hall

These buffs are better than Hero Statue. As with Hero Statue, you can mix ATK and HP increases, or focus on one depending on your needs.

Queue

All buffs are useful except march size increase. We recommend prioritising damage increase and damage reduction villagers.

Statue

Statue villagers aren’t great. Prefer Hero HP and ATK increase. March size increase villagers are usable too, but not optimal.

Guild

Focus on Help Accept Count villagers. They increase the number of helps you can receive, which reduces both building and research times significantly.

Castle

The best buffs are percentage yield boost and construction speed increase. Other buffs are not particularly useful.
